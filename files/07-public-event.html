About this Experience

Join ABCD for an intimate golden-hour photography walk through the historic Cubbon Park. Whether you're a beginner or hobbyist, this walk is designed to help you slow down, observe, and capture Bengaluru's natural light in ways you've never noticed before.

We'll explore shaded forest paths, open lawns, and the ornate Victorian-era buildings that frame the park — each offering completely different light conditions to practice in.
